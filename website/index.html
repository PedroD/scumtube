With ScumTube you can listen to your favorite YouTube musics while performing other tasks or with screen turned off due to the capability of running in the background.
You can download the music that you are listening to your smartphone as a regular .mp3 file.
You have a Music History that allows you to see all the musics that you have played, offering the possibility for you to listen them again.
ScumTube has full integration with the YouTube app.
